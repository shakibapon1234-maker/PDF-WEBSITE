Yes. Antigravity PDF Pro is built as a native Windows desktop client. All conversions (Word, Excel, Images), PDF compares, and OCR reading operations run entirely locally on your processor. We do not host any upload servers, meaning your financial reports and client data never leave your PC.

How does the lifetime license work?

Unlike other PDF editors that charge monthly fees, Antigravity PDF Pro is a one-time purchase. Once you buy a license key through Gumroad, you activate the app once and own it forever. All minor and major updates inside this version are free for life.

What happens after I make the purchase on Gumroad?

Upon completing the secure purchase, Gumroad will instantly email you a unique license key (in the format AGP-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX) along with the download link for the installer. Paste this key into the app's activation box on startup to unlock all premium features forever.

Can I transfer my license to a new computer?
